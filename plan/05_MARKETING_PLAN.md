## 5. CHURN REDUCTION STRATEGY

### 5.1 Churn Prediction
```
Red flags:
- Não criou nota em 7 dias
- Não abriu planner em 14 dias
- Criou apenas 1 nota
- Não respondeu último email

Action:
- Automated email: "We miss you"
- Feature recommendation based on gap
- 20% discount on annual plan
```

### 5.2 Retention Loops
```
Day 1-3: Onboarding (high engagement)
Day 4-7: First habit formation
  → Email: "How are you using Errário?"
  
Day 8-14: Critical period
  → Notification: "See your first insights"
  → Feature unlock: Pizza chart for 5+ notes
  
Day 15+: Engagement check
  → Push: "You haven't created errors this week"
  → Suggestion: Study the chart patterns
```

### 5.3 Win-back Campaign
```
Segment: Inactive > 30 days

Email 1 (Day 31):
Subject: "We've added amazing new features"
Body: Show new integrations (Notion, Anki)

Email 2 (Day 38):
Subject: "See how Emma improved 60% in 2 months"
Body: User testimonial + case study

Email 3 (Day 45):
Subject: "Come back + get 50% off annual"
Body: Last chance offer

Expected: 10-15% win-back rate
```

## 6. CAC & LTV METRICS

### 6.1 Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC)

```
Channel breakdown:

Organic (SEO):
CAC: $0
Expected: 20% of total signups

Referral:
CAC: $2 (credits given)
Expected: 10% of signups

Paid Ads:
CAC: $3-5
Expected: 20% of signups

Content:
CAC: $0 (sweat equity)
Expected: 30% of signups

Community:
CAC: $0
Expected: 20% of signups

Blended CAC Target: < $2
```

### 6.2 Lifetime Value (LTV)

```
Assumptions:
- Average PRO subscription: $9.99/month
- Conversion rate: 15%
- Churn: 5%/month
- Avg lifetime: 20 months

Calculation:
LTV = (ARPU × Gross Margin %) / Churn Rate
LTV = ($9.99 × 70%) / (5% / 100)
LTV = $140

LTV:CAC Ratio = 140 / 2 = 70:1 ✅ (Target: > 3:1)
```
